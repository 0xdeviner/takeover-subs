What is Subdomain Takeover?

Subdomain Takeover occurs when a subdomain points to an external service that is no longer claimed by the original owner.

Steps to Verify:

  1. Identify subdomains pointing to external services.
  2. Check if the service is unclaimed.
  3. Claim the service and deploy your own content.

Proof of Concept:

If you're seeing this page on a vulnerable subdomain, it means the takeover was successful!
